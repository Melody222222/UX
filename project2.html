About:

Nova Pioneer Education Group is a pan - African independent school network offering preschool through secondary education for students from ages 3 through 19, and is a launchpad where ambitious young Africans can begin to shape a better future for the continent and the world.

Like most startups that evolve over time, one of the side effects of this was the antiquated system we had to on board our new and current parents into the School, and something that needed to be looked at as a matter of urgency.

It was not going well for some of our parents…

  • Parents found our online application time consuming and at times abandon the enrollment process altogether due to the confusion and the redundant steps when they had more than one child to enrol.
  • The information for enrollment and fees was not in a central area, parents would struggle to locate fee information.
  • Parents were not given a clear understanding on the enrollment process or timelines, which left users confused and frustrated.
  • There is no shortened application for parents who currently already had children at Nova Pioneer, current parents had to go through this entire process again when enrolling additional children.

My Goal:

  • Our parents are our biggest assets and we needed to make things easier for them.
  • My UX case study would provide feedback for our marketing team on how we can best do so through our website application.
  • This case study is on-going and complete enrollment process would be factored in at a later stage, for now, the aim was to make the website easier as an immediate solve

Some Work Arounds To Consider...

Since most parents only need to go through this process a handful of times, it did prove challenging to gather feedback without having to contact past parents who have long since forgotten their experience.

Parents are inundated with communication from the School regarding internal processes, school events and other school related updates. It was preferred to not engage our parents any further with communication with requests for feedback.

The team who help parents enrol daily did notice a collective theme in concerns parents share, which corroborated the data that we had on hand from usability tests and from our experience in the finance team from what parents have previously shared.

Protoype Results

  • Providing clear instructions on the enrollment process at the very beginning along with fee information.
  • Removing fields that were deemed unnecessary to shorten this process.
  • Advised to update gender pronounces to be more inclusive and include disclaimer for our Personal Protection Act.
  • Ensuring website is inclusive for visually impaired users
  • Current Families only need to update student information
  • Parents can add additional students without needing to complete the entire application process again.
  • A navigation bar was recommended on the homepage to ensure parents have a clear place to find information such as the fee guidelines.
